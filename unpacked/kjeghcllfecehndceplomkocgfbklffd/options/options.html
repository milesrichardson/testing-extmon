Privacy Policy Agreement

Webroot Web Threat Shield Extension for Mozilla Firefox requires your permission to gather information about the webpages you visit (URLs and IP addresses). This is required for our database servers to check the webpage and detect malware. Without this permission the extension will not be able to detect any potentially malicious sites.

By clicking Allow and installing the extension, you agree to forward the visited or searched URL information to Webroot servers to compare URLs in the malware databases.

For more information about what data we are collecting and how we are protecting it, please refer to our Privacy Policy.

Privacy Policy Agreement - Declined

Unfortunately, without your permission to gather the necessary URL information, Web Threat Shield cannot function as intended.

Unless you wish to reconsider the permission to gather this information, we recommend you to remove and uninstall the extension.
